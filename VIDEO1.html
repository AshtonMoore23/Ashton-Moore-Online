 HARRY'S

Making social media video content for Harry's was one of my favorite jobs to date. I loved diving into the world of social media and working with the algorithm to build a bigger following on Instagram and Facebook. Creating content alongside new menu releases and seasonal events like Christmas felt very rewarding. 

 VOSS

Creating video content for their brand was my main focus whilst working for Voss. I was given a brief for each video project, my job was to hit that brief and manage my time efficiently. One of the biggest challenges for me was being realistic about time expectations. I combated this by simplifying my projects and creating detailed schedules to keep me on task.  

 VOLUNTARY WORK

To improve my videography skills I created videos in my spare time. I worked with some amiture artists and models that I met while traveling around Canada. I would use the footage that I captured to learn different editing skills. I used plug in transitions in premiere pro to create more interesting content. This became useful a few years later when working with paying clients.
